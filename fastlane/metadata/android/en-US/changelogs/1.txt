Welcome to Raven — the first release!

• Offline audiobook player for your own M4B, MP3, M4A, OGG and OPUS files
• Automatic folder-based library with Current / Completed / Not Started filters
• Light and dark themes, list and grid layouts
• Playback speed, Volume Boost and Skip Silence
• Bookmarks, smart auto-rewind and accurate position memory
• Sleep timer with end-of-chapter and nightly-window modes (shake to reset)
• Chapter controls in the app and the media notification
• Cover art management and library search
• 100% free, open source, no ads, no tracking
