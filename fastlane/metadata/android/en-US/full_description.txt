Meet Raven — a calm, clutter-free home for your audiobooks.

Raven is a minimalist offline audiobook player for the books you already own. No accounts, no subscriptions, no ads, and no tracking. Just point it at your audio folder and press play.

If you keep your own DRM-free audiobooks (M4B, MP3, M4A, OGG, OGA, OPUS and more), Raven turns them into a tidy, beautiful library. Add your main folder and each subfolder becomes a book automatically — no manual tagging, no cloud, no fuss.

WHY YOU'LL LIKE IT
• 100% offline & ad-free — listen anywhere, no connection required, ever.
• Private by design — Raven collects no personal data and asks for only the permissions it needs to read your files.
• Free & open source (GPLv3) — anyone can inspect exactly what it does.
• A clean, modern interface with full light and dark themes.

BUILT FOR REAL LISTENING
• Remembers your place — every book resumes exactly where you left off.
• Bookmarks — add, rename and manage your own marks, and jump back instantly.
• Chapter controls — skip between chapters from the app or your notification.
• Playback speed, Volume Boost for quiet recordings, and Skip Silence to glide past long pauses.
• Smart auto-rewind — rewinds a few seconds when you resume so you never lose the thread.
• Sleep timer — run for a set time, stop at the end of the chapter, or set a nightly window (e.g. 10 PM–6 AM). Give your phone a shake to restart the timer without unlocking.

YOUR LIBRARY, YOUR WAY
• Search your whole library by title or author in a tap.
• Filter by Current, Completed and Not Started.
• Switch between list and grid layouts.
• Manage cover art — use embedded artwork, pick a local image, or search online for a missing cover.
• Control playback from the home-screen widget and media notification.

Raven is completely free and open source under the GNU GPLv3 license. We respect your privacy and your attention — the app exists to get out of your way so you can enjoy the story.
